About the Stadium

Indore Stadium is a premier sports facility located in the heart of Indore. It serves as a hub for various sports including cricket, football, and athletics. Known for its world-class infrastructure and facilities, the stadium regularly hosts national and international events.

Our Vision

We strive to be a leading venue for sports in India, offering an accessible and professional environment for athletes and fans alike. Our goal is to inspire a passion for sports and provide world-class services for events of all sizes.
